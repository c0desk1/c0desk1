We respect the intellectual property rights of others. This policy explains the procedure for reporting copyright infringement.

## 1. Copyright of Content

All original content on the **c0desk1** website:

- Articles and writings
- Code samples and scripts
- Music and audio
- The `c0desk1` logo

Is the property of **c0desk1** or licensed for use. The content is protected by applicable copyright laws.

## 2. Use of Content by Third Parties

You **are permitted** to:

- Quote small portions of content for review, criticism, or educational purposes
- Include a backlink to the source page

You **are not permitted** to:

- Copy, reproduce, or distribute entire content without permission
- Use the content for commercial purposes without attribution and permission
- Modify or create derivative works without permission

## 3. Infringement Reporting

If you believe that content on this website infringes your copyright, please send a report with the following information:

1. **Identification of the infringed material** (URL or description)
2. **Proof of your copyright ownership**
3. **Your contact information** (name, email, address)
4. A **statement** of good faith that the use is unauthorized
5. Your **signature** (digital or physical)

Send the report to:
**[dmca@c0desk1.my.id](mailto:dmca@c0desk1.my.id)**

## 4. Follow-Up Process

Upon receiving a valid report:

1. We will review the report within 7-14 business days
2. If the violation is confirmed, the content will be removed or access restricted
3. We will notify the user who uploaded the content
4. The user may file an appeal if they believe they are not at fault

## 5. Appeal

If your content was removed due to a DMCA claim and you believe you have the rights, you may file a **counter-notification** including:

- Identification of the removed material
- A statement under penalty of perjury that you have the rights
- Consent to court jurisdiction

## 6. Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to terminate the access of users who repeatedly infringe the copyright of others.

For questions related to copyright:
