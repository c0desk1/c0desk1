By using **c0desk1**, you agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

### 1. Data We Collect
We collect the following data to operate and improve the service:

| Data Type | Examples | Collection Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Technical Data** | IP address, browser type, device | Automatically via analytics |
| **Usage Data** | Pages visited, time spent | Automatically via analytics |
| **Communication Data** | Email, messages, comments | When you submit a form |
| **Cookies** | Theme preferences, user sessions | Automatically via your browser |

### 2. How We Use Data
We use data to:
- Provide and maintain the website
- Analyze performance and usage
- Improve content and user experience
- Respond to your inquiries
- Display relevant ads through Google AdSense and Yandex Ads

### 3. Third-Party Services
We use third-party services that may process your data:

| Service | Purpose | Data Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Google Analytics** | Traffic analysis | Google servers |
| **Yandex Metrica** | Traffic analysis | Yandex servers |
| **Google AdSense** | Advertising | Google servers |
| **Yandex Ads** | Advertising | Yandex servers |
| **Cloudflare** | CDN & security | Cloudflare servers |

Each service has its own privacy policy.

### 4. Cookies
We use cookies to store theme preferences, analyze traffic, and serve ads.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

### 5. Advertising
Ads are served by Google AdSense and Yandex Ads based on content and user behavior.

Manage your ad preferences at:
- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Yandex Ad Settings](https://yandex.com/support/ad-settings/)

### 6. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data.

DATA IS TRANSMITTED AT YOUR OWN RISK. NO METHOD OF INTERNET TRANSMISSION IS 100% SECURE.

### 7. Children’s Privacy
This service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If you believe we have such data, contact us to have it deleted.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Restrict or object to data processing
- Withdraw consent at any time

To make a request, contact us through the [Contact](/contact/) page.
