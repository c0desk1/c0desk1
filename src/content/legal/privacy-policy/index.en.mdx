We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the **c0desk1** website collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

## 1. Information Collected

We may collect the following information:

| Type of Data | Example | Collection Method |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Technical data** | IP address, browser type, device | Automatically (via analytics) |
| **Usage data** | Pages visited, time of visit | Automatically (via analytics) |
| **Communication data** | Email, messages, comments | Contact or comment forms |
| **Cookies** | User preferences | Automatically (via browser) |

## 2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Provide and operate the website
- Analyze website performance and usage
- Improve content and user experience
- Respond to your questions or comments
- Display relevant ads (Google AdSense / Yandex Ads)

## 3. Third Parties

We use third-party services that may collect your data:

| Service | Function | Data Location |
| -------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------- |
| **Google Analytics** | Traffic analysis | Google servers |
| **Yandex Metrica** | Traffic analysis | Yandex servers |
| **Google AdSense** | Advertising | Google servers |
| **Yandex Ads** | Advertising | Yandex servers |
| **Cloudflare** | CDN & security | Cloudflare servers |
| **GitHub** | Repository hosting | GitHub servers |

Third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend that you read them.

## 4. Cookies

This website uses **cookies** to:

- Save theme preferences (dark/light mode)
- Analyze visitor traffic
- Display relevant ads

You can set your browser to refuse cookies. However, some website features may not function optimally.

## 5. Advertising

This website displays ads from:

- **Google AdSense**
- **Yandex Ads**

Ads are displayed based on content and user behavior. We do not control the specific ads that appear.

You can manage ad preferences via:

- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Yandex Ad Settings](https://yandex.com/support/ad-settings/)

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, **no method of data transmission is 100% secure**.

We are not responsible for unauthorized access by third parties beyond our control.

## 7. Children's Data

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe we have data of a child under 13, please contact us.
