Being a microstock contributor in this day and age isn't just about photo aesthetics. It's about **SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**. You can have a crystal-clear photo, but without the right title and 50 keywords, your photo will just languish in a digital warehouse, never to be purchased.

The problem is one: **Manual metadata input is tedious and time-consuming.**

That's why I built **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)**. An idealistic project born from personal concerns, designed to be a personal assistant for every contributor in Indonesia.

---

## Contributor Bottleneck

Imagine you've just come home from a *gym* or *industrial* photo shoot and have 100 photos that have been thoroughly *edited*. For each photo, you have to think about:

- A descriptive but not *spammy* title.
- 50 relevant keywords.
- A category that meets Adobe Stock standards (there are 21 specific categories).

If one photo takes 3-5 minutes, you'll need **8 hours just for the text!** This is where **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)** comes in to cut that time down to less than **10 seconds per photo**.

## Why Is Adogen Different? 

I built [Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/) as more than just a regular AI wrapper. I made several technical decisions to ensure "Burag" :V developer-like performance:

### Astro 6 & Tailwind 4
Using the latest technology to ensure First Contentful Paint (FCP) times under 1 second. Lightweight, minimalist, and incredibly fast.

### Vision AI Integration
Adogen uses the Llama 3.2 Vision and Pixtral models. This AI doesn't guess based on file names, but actually "sees" the objects, colors, atmosphere, and concepts in your images.

### Client-Side Processing
Your images are resized in the browser before being sent to the API. This saves you data and speeds up uploads.

### CSV Batch Export
Once finished, you don't have to copy and paste them one by one.  One click of a button, and the CSV file is ready to import into your Adobe Stock dashboard.

## The "Free But Premium" Philosophy

Many similar tools out there charge a hefty monthly subscription price. Adogen takes a different approach: Bring Your Own API Key.

By using an API key from Groq or Mistral (which has a very generous/loyal free tier), you have complete control over your costs and privacy. No image data is permanently stored—everything is processed in real-time.

## How to Start the Workflow of the Future

<Steps>
### Prepare Your API Key
Get your free API key at **[Groq](https://console.groq.com/)** or **[Mistral AI](https://console.mistral.ai/)**.

### Configuration
Enter the key in the **System Configuration** panel of **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)**.

### Upload & Analyze
Put all your photos in, and watch the AI work to assemble the metadata for you.

### Export
Download the CSV, upload it to Adobe Stock, and get back to shooting!
</Steps>
