This spring season is truly something special. It's not just about the blooming flowers, but the amazing lineup of anime ready to accompany our downtime. From long-awaited sequels to brand new stories that immediately steal the spotlight—April 2026 feels like enjoying a buffet with the best dishes you wouldn't want to miss.

![Anime Spring 2026 Must-Add to Your Watchlist!](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMjafITvsqFNEbzvOQZLbOmbS5ZfsAAArgOaxsYJZFXkJROSLix-4sBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

If you're confused about where to start or don't want to miss what's trending, our team has put together these Spring 2026 anime recommendations in a relaxed, easy-to-digest manner. Let's jump right in and check out the list!

## Legendary Sequels You've Been Dying to Watch

This season could be called a "feast of kings" because so many major series are returning. If you're eager to see the continuation of your favorite stories, here they are.

### 1. One Piece: Elbaf Arc
![One Piece: Elbaf Arc | caption:One Piece: Elbaf Arc ](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMlafIZOWh1YlJTguiXHytwDu1XuBYAAr4OaxsYJZFXZSrNHrQjhKgBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

A season wouldn't be complete without the adventures of the Straw Hat pirates. This time, after being promised for so long, Luffy and his crew finally reach **Elbaf, the legendary land of giants**! Aren't you curious about Usopp's reaction, who has dreamed of going here since forever? This arc will also introduce Prince Loki and hold major secrets about the world's history. The good news is, the animation quality has been upgraded because the series has switched to a seasonal format for better quality. Airing from **April 5, 2026**, this is a must-watch!

### 2. Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World Season 4
![Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World Season 4 | caption:Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World Season 4](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMnafIZsGwfy0IeH5qsrYWhL8s_6HIAAsIOaxsYJZFX43Jw_GDTZ4gBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Brace yourselves, suffering lovers! Subaru is back with new levels of torment in its fourth season, which is rumored to be the darkest yet. Adapting the "Loss" arc, this season will test Subaru's psyche even deeper. Staying consistent, this anime still delivers psychological thrills that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Airing from **April 8, 2026**.

### 3. That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime (Tensura) Season 4
![That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime (Tensura) Season 4 | caption:That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime (Tensura) Season 4](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMpafIZ0pNqdASiHby5-96Qw6q_rXwAAsMOaxsYJZFXGIMNOWVGoeYBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Who hasn't missed Rimuru the slime demon? In this fourth season, Rimuru's newly established kingdom will face formidable enemies, including powerful figures from the Demon Lord ranks. So highly anticipated, in Japan this anime even ranked first as the most anticipated anime according to an Animate Times poll. We'll witness how a former salaryman reincarnated as a slime builds an ideal civilization amidst political intrigue and fierce battles. Airing from **April 3, 2026**.

### 4. Classroom of the Elite Season 4: Second Year Arc
![Classroom of the Elite Season 4: Second Year Arc | caption:Classroom of the Elite Season 4: Second Year Arc](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMrafIaAAEjMWG_P_k9Tt9i583rFF6BAALFDmsbGCWRV9CSJp2kgCt8AQADAgADeQADOwQ)

Plot twists, intrigue, and manipulation. This series is truly a champion of mind games. The fourth season begins adapting the second-year arc, which promises to be even more intense as Ayanokoji now has to deal with upperclassmen as well as the new batch of students beneath him. The appearance of a mysterious new student also from the White Room makes the conflict even more heated. For those who love anime with a mysterious genius protagonist, this is your pick. Starts **April 1, 2026**.

## Brand New Adaptations You Must Watch

Besides sequels, Spring 2026 also welcomes various new arrivals of no less quality. Some of them might become your new favorites.

### 5. Dorohedoro Season 2
![Dorohedoro S2 | caption:Dorohedoro Season 2](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMtafIaSot16DP4hKxVVKLRUdaqCysAAscOaxsYJZFX_5uaGTHARJABAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

After six long years, this brutal yet quirky anime mixing magic and demons is finally back! Produced by MAPPA, the second season continues Caiman and Nikaido's adventures in the grimy, dark world. Rough, bold animation combined with its characteristic humor gives Dorohedoro a special place in fans' hearts. It's a dark fantasy genre with violent scenes, so minors need parental guidance. Airing **April 1, 2026** on Crunchyroll.

### 6. Witch Hat Atelier
![Witch Hat Atelier | caption:Witch Hat Atelier](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMvafIaZ5CQvYiPkmJcSvyyYk4ticAAAsgOaxsYJZFXLYU-tB5HqEABAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

For those who long for a fantasy anime with elegant magic and stunning visuals, *Witch Hat Atelier* is the answer. The story follows Coco, an ordinary girl who accidentally discovers a major secret of the magical world. Adapted from Kamome Shirahama's Eisner Award-winning manga, this anime is guaranteed to have deep storytelling and visuals that please the eyes. Get ready to enter a world full of mystery and unexpected danger. Airing **April 6, 2026** and already on many media's "must-watch" lists.

### 7. Steel Ball Run (JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure)
![Steel Ball Run (JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure) | caption:Steel Ball Run (JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure)](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMxafIagRQ_CIbt34Th0DNMS0kZ-fwAAsoOaxsYJZFXJgT0XyXTvbUBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Great news for JoJo fans! The adaptation of one of the most epic arcs, Steel Ball Run, finally airs this season. Different from the previous timeline, this story brings Johnny Joestar and Gyro Zeppeli into a crazy, mysterious cross-America race. Handled by David Production, known for its faithfulness to Hirohiko Araki's original style, the first special episode even runs for 47 minutes. Get ready for a different kind of nostalgia with Stands and adventure! Airing **March 19, 2026** on Netflix.

### 8. Liar Game
![Liat Game | caption:Liar Game](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAMzafIaopdfrREOfvhDGDZHDh4tJG4AAssOaxsYJZFXq2KZUiIu2f0BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

For fans of psychological thrillers who love to think hard, *Liar Game* could be the most enjoyable choice. This classic manga finally gets an anime adaptation after a long wait. The story follows Nao Kanzaki, an honest girl trapped in a massive manipulation game, who then gets help from a genius named Akinoya Shinichi. Prepare your mind, because every episode will truly test our logic. Available starting **April 6, 2026** on Crunchyroll.

### 9. The Ramparts of Ice (Koori no Jouheki)
![The Ramparts of Ice (Koori no Jouheki) | caption:The Ramparts of Ice (Koori no Jouheki)](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAM1afIaz9pMTbUvl6o6Y28XsENwWQ4AAswOaxsYJZFX9IJEt2J3zXwBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Bored of the same old romance dramas? Try *The Ramparts of Ice*. This anime tells the story of Koyuki Hikawa, a high school girl nicknamed the "Ice Queen" because of her cold demeanor. Set in a unique world with realistic teenage romance and drama, similar to *You and I Are Polar Opposites*. Airing **April 2, 2026**, this is a perfect recommendation for those who love feel-good, emotional slice-of-life stories.

### 10. Snowball Earth
![Snowball Earth | caption:Snowball Earth](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAM3afIa6HAHn_QgfCK-6K6FgI0AAVpjAALNDmsbGCWRVwp80cD6BGoyAQADAgADeQADOwQ)

Another sci-fi thriller not to be missed. Imagine a man waking up from a long sleep to find the entire Earth frozen into a "snowball". He must survive in this extreme environment while uncovering the mystery behind the catastrophe. Not many spoilers, but the trailer alone is enough to spark curiosity. Airing **April 3, 2026**, this is an interesting watch for post-apocalyptic genre fans.

### 11. Isekai Nonbiri Nouka (Farming Life in Another World) Season 2
![Isekai Nonbiri Nouka (Farming Life in Another World) Season 2 | caption:Isekai Nonbiri Nouka (Farming Life in Another World) Season 2](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAAM5afIbCAsIgr1BZDr-P5yYBOC4FsAAAs4OaxsYJZFX92_nIg_qpz0BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

When it comes to a relaxing life in another world, this title truly takes the crown. Unlike typical action-packed isekai, here we get to experience the joy of clearing land, planting, and building a village from scratch. No pressure, no burdens—just peace and pure laughter that will warm your heart.

The first season successfully branded itself as stress-relieving viewing, especially with the antics of the increasingly lively village residents. In this second season, the story continues Hiraku's adventures with the residents of the Giant Tree Village, whose daily life grows even more vibrant.

## Take It Easy: Tips for Enjoying a Packed Anime Season

Facing over 90 new titles airing this season can indeed be overwhelming. My advice: don't rush. Just pick 3–5 titles from the recommendations above that best suit your favorite genres. For example:

- **Love magical fantasy?** Don't miss *Witch Hat Atelier*.
- **Love action and intense battles?** *Dorohedoro* or *Tensura* is the answer.
- **Love puzzles and manipulation?** *Classroom of the Elite* and *Liar Game* are the main courses.
- **Love a calm, heartwarming fantasy world that makes you smile to yourself?**
  *Isekai Nonbiri Nouka Season 2* is the answer. Perfect to watch while relaxing after a tiring day.

Amidst the action and intrigue of **Classroom of the Elite** or the harshness of **Dorohedoro**'s world, **Isekai Nonbiri Nouka Season 2** can be a refreshing oasis. This farming slice of life anime promises a warm and entertaining viewing experience. Watching Hiraku and his unique family in the village feels like coming home after a long journey.

Ultimately, there's nothing wrong with simply relaxing and enjoying each episode accompanied by your favorite snacks. That's the true essence of watching anime, isn't it?

## Conclusion

This spring season is truly special. The combination of returning legends *One Piece, Re:Zero, Slime* and spectacular new adaptations *Dorohedoro, JoJo, Isekai Nonbiri Nouka* creates a classy lineup. For true anime fans, this is a highly anticipated moment.
