## Hey, fellow Android gamer!

Tired of the same old mainstream titles clogging up your phone's memory? It's time to turn your attention to the world of <mark>**indie games**</mark>. This April 2026, there's a fantastic wave of creative, unique, and lightweight games just waiting to be played.

This list will guide you through 7 of the best indie titles you can download and play right now. They're guaranteed to be fun, unique, and easy on both your wallet and your phone's storage. Let's dive in!

---

## Sea of Stars

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANDafMhwLjaTKPrPP0t1J7C5sU530gAAtEQaxsYJZlXjao36G0Kbo8BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

For fans of classic JRPGs like *Chrono Trigger*, *Sea of Stars* is an absolute must-try. This title from Sabotage Studio, already a hit on consoles and PC, officially arrived on Android on **April 7, 2026**.

The game features dynamic turn-based combat, a rich and captivating story, and seamless exploration with a touch of nostalgia. Even better, the mobile version is fully optimized with an intuitive touch interface and full controller support for maximum comfort. Priced around **$10.99 USD**, it's well worth it for a high-quality RPG experience.

## Drova - Forsaken Kin

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANFafMiAAEicWJSdEQy4GhQOO3xYcuQAALTEGsbGCWZV3Y2M3UGEScDAQADAgADeQADOwQ)

Still in the *action RPG* genre, *Drova - Forsaken Kin* offers a completely different vibe. Set in a grim world inspired by **Celtic mythology**, visualized through stunning pixel art, this game feels like a pocket-sized version of a classic *Gothic* title.

The decisions you make will directly shape the story and its ending. *Drova* was officially released on **April 23, 2026** on the Google Play Store, priced at €9.99 (around **$10.99 USD**) with full controller support right from the start.

## Neverness to Everness

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANHafMiIqcbd02NQkyAOFuiHNHtLzQAAtQQaxsYJZlXESO-pBm-4D0BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Bored of regular *open-world* games? *Neverness to Everness* (NTE) might be the answer. Dubbed "**Anime GTA**" by some media, this game drops you into the futuristic city of Hethereau to become an "Anomaly Hunter".

The freedom in this game is incredible. You can explore the city, race through the streets in custom cars, buy properties, or even start businesses. The coolest feature? This game from Hotta Studio supports *cross-progression*, meaning you can continue your progress on PC or PS5! It launched on **April 29, 2026**, and there was even talk of a free character as a launch gift for early players.

## Gambonanza

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANJafMiReFamhfZ9jXx8dQNLSAiZ0wAAtUQaxsYJZlXxQABFGGj18gDAQADAgADeQADOwQ)

Can't decide between a game of chess and a *roguelike* dungeon crawl? *Gambonanza* mashes them both together. Created by a solo developer, this game is essentially a **chess-based roguelike**.

At first glance, it might look like a *Mario Party* board, but don't be fooled—the tactical depth and strategic challenge are immense. Though it just hit Android at the end of April (May 1, 2026), it quickly became one of the most anticipated titles due to its incredibly unique concept amidst a sea of AAA games. Perfect for those who love a good mental workout.

## The Rhymatory

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANLafMibke6nFu4UHOPUh-L3LGVlcUAAtYQaxsYJZlXziyrQvKO5h8BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

If you're looking for a relaxing way to unwind after a long day, *The Rhymatory* is the perfect pick. This word puzzle game presents you with over **300 rhyming puzzles** waiting to be solved.

It's set in a cozy *steampunk* world where you're accompanied by a friendly ruby, an owl, and a crow who are ready to brew you some tea. Released on **April 30, 2026**, the game is wonderfully stress-free, with no pressure at all—and it's incredibly wallet-friendly, as it's **free to play**.

## Hearthside: A Teeny Tiny Game

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANNafMijZ2kGqbm9rA-BkXiYFSplZYAAtcQaxsYJZlXMcjn_ien9xIBAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

For lovers of cozy games like *Animal Crossing* or *Stardew Valley*, *Hearthside: A Teeny Tiny Game* will make you feel right at home. This *puzzle* and *city-builder* game asks you to carefully place tiles to construct a charming little town.

Even though it seems simple, the gameplay is surprisingly addictive. You need to cleverly plan your tile placement to help your town grow. If your plan fails, there's no penalty—just start again! It's the perfect game for filling your free time at home.

## Annulus

![](https://cdn.c0desk1.my.id/img/AgACAgUAAxkBAANPafMiqp_gJhN8XDZEPksgPWKyHXUAAtgQaxsYJZlXSkav0C6jS-8BAAMCAAN5AAM7BA)

Lastly, for those who crave a tactical challenge. *Annulus* is a **strategy RPG** set in a terrifying *dark fantasy* world. You'll lead a legion of mercenaries across the bleak lands of Novisess to survive and uncover its dark secrets.

One of its main attractions is the **Unreal Engine**-powered graphics, which give it an incredibly cinematic look for a mobile strategy game. Released on **April 8, 2026**, this is a must-try for any fan of strategy and tactics.
