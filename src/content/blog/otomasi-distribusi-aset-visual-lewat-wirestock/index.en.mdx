Generating aesthetic *AI images* is only half the battle. The real challenge is distribution. Uploading assets one by one to **Adobe Stock**, **Shutterstock**, and **Pond5**, complete with descriptions and dozens of tags, is a tedious, repetitive process.

This is where **Wirestock** comes in as a one-stop distribution solution. This article will explore how I leverage this platform to cut down on operational time and focus entirely on the creative process.

## One Dashboard for All Markets

Wirestock's main concept is to be an aggregator. You simply upload once, and your assets will be automatically distributed to various major microstock platforms.

- **Auto-Tagging**: You don't have to worry about keywords. Their AI will curate relevant metadata to make your photos easily discoverable by global buyers.
- **Global Reach**: Your assets immediately have the opportunity to sell on platforms you might otherwise be too lazy to manually list individually.

## Resolution and Curation

Although the process is automated, the quality of the input remains your responsibility. Wirestock has quite strict curation standards:

- **Upscaling**: Make sure your AI images have been upscaled to at least 4K resolution. Image distortion will be immediately rejected.
- **AI Labeling**: Always be honest about whether your images are the result of Generative AI. Transparency is key to ensuring your account's long-term security.

## Monetization Scheme and Cuts

Let's talk logically. Wirestock takes a commission of around 15% of your sales.

You exchange that 15% of sales for hundreds of hours of your valuable time.

For creators who pursue quantity while maintaining quality, this system is far more profitable than having to manage 5-10 stock platform accounts independently.

## Do What's Important, Automate the Rest

For me, Wirestock isn't just a place to sell photos, but an automation tool. With this system, I can focus on researching new niches and experimenting with prompts without being bogged down by tedious administrative tasks.
