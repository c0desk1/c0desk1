Being a microstock contributor today is no longer just about visual quality. It's an **SEO (Search Engine Optimization)** game. You could have a photo with perfect detail, but without the right title and 50 highly relevant keywords, your asset will simply sink in a sea of content — never to be found, let alone purchased.

The problem is clear: **writing metadata manually is incredibly tedious and time-consuming.**

That's why **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)** was born — a project driven by personal experience, designed to be a smart assistant for every contributor in Indonesia.

![Bridging human creativity with machine efficiency.](/images/blog/hero-adogen.webp)

## The Contributor's Bottleneck

After a photoshoot—say, a *gym* or *industrial* theme—and you have 100 fully edited photos, you still have to prepare:

- A descriptive but not *spammy* title.
- 50 truly relevant keywords.
- A category that fits Adobe Stock's standards (there are 21 specific categories).

If one photo takes 3–5 minutes for text work, you'll need **over 8 hours just for metadata**. This is where **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)** steps in, slashing that time down to less than **10 seconds per image**.

## What Makes Adogen Different?

I didn't build **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)** as just another AI *wrapper*. There are a few technical decisions I made in the name of maximum performance:

### Astro 6 & Tailwind 4
Adopting a modern stack to guarantee a *First Contentful Paint* (FCP) under 1 second. Lightweight, minimal, and blazing fast.

### Vision AI Integration
Adogen utilizes the **Llama 3.2 Vision** and **Pixtral** models. The AI doesn't guess based on the filename; it actually "sees" the objects, colors, mood, and concepts within your image.

### Client-Side Processing
Images are resized right in the browser before being sent to the API. This saves your data quota and speeds up the entire process.

### CSV Batch Export
Once finished, you don't need to copy and paste one by one. A single click prepares a CSV file ready to be imported into your Adobe Stock dashboard.

## The "Free but Premium" Philosophy

Many similar services charge a hefty monthly subscription fee. **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)** takes a different approach: **Bring Your Own API Key.**

By leveraging an API key from **[Groq](https://console.groq.com/)** or **[Mistral](https://console.mistral.ai/)** (both of which offer a very generous free tier), you have full control over costs and privacy. No image data is permanently stored — everything is processed in *real-time*.

## Starting the Workflow

:::steps
### Prepare Your API Key
Get your free API key at **[Groq](https://console.groq.com/)** or **[Mistral AI](https://console.mistral.ai/)**.

### Configuration
Enter the key in the **System Configuration** panel of **[Adogen](https://dev-adogen.bimasaktiakbarr.workers.dev/)**.

### Upload & Analyze
Upload all your photos, and watch the AI assemble the metadata for you.

### Export
Download the CSV, upload it to Adobe Stock, and get back to shooting!
:::
